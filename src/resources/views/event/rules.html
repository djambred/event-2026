Submission Guidelines

  • 01
    Originality Requirement

    All work must be original and not previously published or awarded in other competitions.

  • 02
    Video Standards

    Minimum 1080p resolution, landscape orientation, with clear audio and no copyright-infringing background music.

  • 03
    Digital Artwork File

    High-resolution PDF or JPEG (minimum 300 DPI) for visual submissions, accompanied by a 300-word concept description.
